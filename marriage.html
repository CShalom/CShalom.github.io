Words of Wisdom: Love & Marriage

"A husband and wife may disagree on many things but they must absolutely agree on this: to never, ever give up.” – Unknown

"Never marry the one you can live with, marry the one you cannot live without."—Unknown

"A successful marriage requires falling in love many times, always with the same person."– Mignon McLaughlin

"A great marriage is not when the ‘perfect couple’ comes together. It is when an imperfect couple learns to enjoy their differences."– Dave Meurer

"Marriage is not a noun; it is a verb. It is not something you get. It is something you do. It is the way you love your partner every day."– Barbara De Angelis

"Don’t ever stop dating your wife and don’t ever stop flirting with your husband."– Unknown

"A perfect marriage is just two imperfect people who refuse to give up on each other."– Unknown

"The highest happiness on earth is marriage."— William Lyon Phelps

"A happy marriage is the union of two good forgivers."— Ruth Bell Graham

"Being someone’s first love may be great, but to be their last is beyond perfect."—Anonymous

"Love doesn’t make the world go round; love is what makes the ride worthwhile."—Elizabeth Barrett Browning

"There are a hundred paths through the world that are easier than loving. But who wants easier?"—Marry Oliver

"I love you not only for what you are, but for what I am when I am with you."—Roy Croft

"The secret of a happy marriage remains a secret."—Henny Youngman

"You don’t stop loving a person when they’re hurt. You get stronger so they can lean on you." —Kristin Hannahweee

"A good marriage is a contest of generosity."—Diane Sawyer

"Happy is the man who finds a true friend, and far happier is he who finds that true friend in his wife."—Franz Schubert
